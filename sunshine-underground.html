SUNSHINE UNDERGROUND

 

For the Dan Wallace Scholarship for the School of Communication Arts 2.0, I was asked to bring them sunshine. I wanted to inject a little happiness and positive social interaction in a place where there usually is none - the tube.

 

The idea started with me thinking about the RFID tech in oyster cards. What if you could high 5 someone with your oyster card to tap in? Then it just snowballed into this:

 

  • Motivational posters along the escalator to lift your spirits
  • Complimentary cuppa and a biccie while you wait on the platform
  • Uplifting music and conversation in the carriage to put you in a winning frame of mind
