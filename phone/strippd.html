Client: STRIPPD Protein Powder

Social Video Script

 

INT. KITCHEN - EVENING:

 

POV

Sarah walks into the kitchen, looking for something to eat while her partner is meal-prepping an inhuman amount of brown food (boiled chicken and brown rice). Tupperware is strewn all over the counters. The woman opens the fridge and picks out greenery, other colourful vegetables, hummus and salmon to make a meal.

 

VO

“I’m starving! Better get some lunch before Dan’s buried in a brown food tupperware avalanche.”

 

INT. KITCHEN - DAY:

 

POV

Dan is preparing a brown protein shake with loud and obnoxious packaging (‘Ultra Mass Heavy Weight Gain Shake’). The tub of ‘brotein’ is absolutely huge (5kg at least). He struggles with opening the shaker, but when he finally gets it open, he is overcome with an awful smell from inside the bottle. He shakes the bottle vigorously for quite a long time (he looks tired).

Sarah looks down and we see her preparing a variety of colourful, fresh fruit. The

STRIPPD bottle is next to her fruit and a blender filled with some ice. We see her finish preparing her shake (it looks colourful and vibrant. As she says her last line, she takes a sip of the shake.

 

VO

“Dan says he has to shake it like that to stop it being powdery. I feel sorry for him sometimes... I like to keep it simple.”

 

END PLATE: WHY FIT IN WHEN YOU CAN STAND OUT?
